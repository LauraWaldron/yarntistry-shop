About me:

Hi! My name is Laura and I've been crocheting for about 7 years. I love creating custom dolls, stuffed animals, and toys for charity, for sale, or for enjoyment. I especially love using different colors, styles, and eyes to make my creations super cute. Thanks for joining me on my journey. :)

My Products:

My specialty is the Goombie, an adorable baby-Yoda-style key chain with large dark eyes, ears, and hands. Hang him on your backpack or clip him on your purse! I also crochet numerous dogs, cats, bears, dolls, fruit, sweet treats, & key chains.
